## ✅ Requirements

The mount had to tick three boxes:

- **Canyon cockpit compatible** — fits H31, CP01, CP04, CP06, CP07, CP10 & CP16 aero/ergo bars
- **GoPro-style quick release** — works with GoPro and compatible devices like front lights
- **Stiff, light, and aero** — no wobbly plastic brackets ruining the clean bar profile
